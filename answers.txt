Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer:
There are multiple different reasons to use git:
1. Collaboration with others on projects is made easier and more efficent.
2. Different versions of the repository's history and its commits (saves) are stored in case of situations
   where there is a need to revert or access code that was since altered.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Rebasing makes the branches visually cleaner to look at and more linear, but preserves less details on commit history. 
        Merging keeps all of the saved details/merges. Merge history can become messy to look at as there are more branches
